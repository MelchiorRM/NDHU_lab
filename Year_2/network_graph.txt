Question 1: Who is the Most Popular?
Description

In a social network, we can consider "people"asVerticesand "friendships" asEdges. Assume friendships are bidirectional (Undirected Graph).

Please write a program to read the social network data and calculate the number of friends each person has. In graph theory, this is called calculating theDegreeof each vertex.


Input

The first line contains two integers

N and M
M, representing the total number of people (vertices) and relationships (edges). People are numbered from 1 to N.

The next M lines each contain two integers u and v, indicating that person u and person v are friends.


Output N lines. The i-th line should contain a single integer representing the number of friends (degree) of person i.
sample input: 
4 3
1 2
2 3
1 4
sample output:
2
2
1
1
